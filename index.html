About Us

We are proud to be the third batch of Noorul Huda Islamic Academy, affectionately known as the Taiba Batch. Our journey began on July 7, 2017, when we stepped into this noble institution, carrying dreams in our hearts and faith in our souls. What started as individual journeys soon became a shared path of growth, unity, and transformation. Over the years, Noorul Huda has shaped us not only through knowledge but through values, discipline, and spiritual insight. It became more than just a place of studyit became a second home. A place where we learned the meanings of brotherhood and respect, where we found mentors who guided us with wisdom, and friends who became family. Our time here was filled with countless momentsclasses that enlightened us, events that united us, and memories that will remain etched in our hearts. As we look back, we carry forward the legacy of Noorul Huda with gratitude and purpose. We are, and will always be, the Taiba Batcha family forged in faith and destined to shine..
